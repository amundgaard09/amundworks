## **Projects**

### **SENTINEL***
***The Advanced All-Environment Exosuit Platform designed to extend human capability beyond natural limits.***

*What is SENTINEL?*

**SENTINEL** is an experimental powered exosuit platform developed to explore the frontier between human ability and machine augmentation. Designed as a modular system combining robotics, wearable mechanics and advanced human-machine interfaces, **SENTINEL** aims to enhance strength, endurance, mobility and situational awareness in extreme environments.

Built as a long-term engineering project under *AmundWorks*, **SENTINEL** serves as a testbed for future technologies in powered exoskeletons, robotics and wearable systems. 

With **SENTINEL**, you get:
- A modular powered exoskeleton designed to enhance human strength, mobility and endurance.

- An integrated helmet and HUD system for real-time information, navigation and communications.

- A complete platform of sensors to ensure complete situational awareness.

- A development platform for advanced robotics, propulsion experiments and life-support technologies.

- **ICARUS**, AmundWorks' own operational and tactical assistant, in charge of ensuring proper and safe use of **SENTINEL**.

### **PRODIGY**
***The All-In-One Management Assistant for Student-Athletes.***

*What is **Prodigy?***

**PRODIGY** was made to make the lives of student athletes easier. It makes planning daily activities, schoolwork and training easier with flexible, customizable and potent management software. 

With **PRODIGY**, you get:
- A complete set of tools for planning, executing and documenting training, called **OLYMPUS**.
- An equally complete set of tools for planning schoolwork, called **ORION**.
- A powerful management suite, made for workflow and ease of use, comprised of **OLYMPUS** and **ORION**.
